• BLOG: ‘NCIS’ Star Pauley Perrette Gets Special Thanks From Swaziland

    Thirst Project is lucky to have a lot of celebrities on board with our cause, but few are as dedicated as “NCIS” beauty Pauley Perrette. Now she’s getting recognized personally for her generous donations towards our efforts in Swaziland!

    The actress, who plays quirky Abby Sciutoon the show, has not been shy about speaking out about Thirst Project and pitching in from her own wallet to help build wells all over the world. At left, you can see her at last year’s Thirst Gala. In a recent video, Swazi women show what they used to do by gathering dirty water from a nearby stream. Then, it’s smiles and laughter all around as fresh, clean h10 sprays out of their new well. The people of Nkamanzi, Swaziland say “Thank you, Pauley P.” and so do we!

  • MTV’s “Teen Wolf” Cast Funds Uganda Well

    The Cast of MTV’s “Teen Wolf” funded this Thirst Project fresh water well at the Bethlehem Parents School and Orphanage in Rakai, Uganda. This video was created by The Thirst Project Media Team to report back to the cast on their completed well, and shows the school’s water source before the well was built, as well as the finished project.
