    BLOG: ‘NCIS’ Star Pauley Perrette Gets Special Thanks From Swaziland

    Thirst Project is lucky to have a lot of celebrities on board with our cause, but few are as dedicated as “NCIS” beauty Pauley Perrette. Now she’s getting recognized personally for her generous donations towards our efforts in Swaziland!

    The actress, who plays quirky Abby Sciuto on the show, has not been shy about speaking out about Thirst Project and pitching in from her own wallet to help build wells all over the world. At left, you can see her at last year’s Thirst Gala.

    In a recent video, Swazi women show what they used to do by gathering dirty water from a nearby stream. Then, it’s smiles and laughter all around as fresh, clean H20 sprays out of their new well. The people of Nkamanzi, Swaziland say “Thank you, Pauley P.” and so do we!
